About Ronaldo

He was born on February 5, 1985, on the island of Madeira, Portugal. His father worked as a kit man at a local club, and Ronaldo began playing football at an early age,Ronaldo joined the youth team of Sporting CP in Lisbon at age 12 and quickly rose through the ranks, eventually making his senior team debut at age 16...

Ronaldo has a strong work ethic and is driven to achieve his goals,He is known for his self-assured demeanor and believes in his abilities,He has a strong desire to win and is always pushing himself to be the best,He is active in philanthropic efforts, supporting causes such as education and children's health....

In 2003, Ronaldo was signed by Manchester United, and quickly became a key player for the club, winning numerous trophies and awards, In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid for a record-breaking transfer fee, and went on to become the club's all-time leading goalscorer, winning four Champions League titles and numerous other trophies,Ronaldo has represented Portugal at numerous international tournaments, winning the 2016 European Championship and becoming the country's all-time leading goalscorer.
